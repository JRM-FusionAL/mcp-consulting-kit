## Execution Rules
1. No custom work before paid pilot scope is signed.
2. Every demo maps to one business outcome and one KPI.
3. Keep first pilots narrow: one buyer, one workflow, one success metric.
4. Productize repeated custom asks into the next kit release.
5. Weekly review: drop low-converting channels, double down on high-signal channels.
